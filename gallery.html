About Us

Olopo is a smart reward ecosystem designed to turn every transaction into a rewarding experience. From local stores to big brands, users earn cashback and points on every purchase—redeemable for shopping, bill payments, ticket bookings, and more. Backed by HDFC for secure transactions, Olopo empowers users with financial freedom, loyalty benefits, and seamless digital shopping. Our goal is to revolutionize everyday spending through a powerful mix of tech, trust, and rewards. With features like scratch cards, referrals, and real-time savings tracking, Olopo is more than an app—it’s your daily companion for smarter spending.
