What are My Ads?

My Ads is Olopo’s in-app advertising tool designed to help businesses big or small get noticed easily.

Do You Have a Product or Service to Sell?.

My Ads lets you advertise your product or service directly to Olopo app users starting at only ₹1 per day!

Whether you're selling a product, service, or offer, you can
  • Upload your product or service details
  • Create a free catalogue directly on the Olopo platform
  • Let customers purchase using their Olopo reward points.
Ad Categories You Can Choose From
  • Direct Purchase Link – Instantly redirect users to buy.
  • Product to App – Drive attention to your listed items in the Olopo app
  • Order Placed via App – Encourage direct purchases right through the platform
How to Use My Ads
  • Upload your product or service
  • Create your free catalogue on the Olopo platform
  • Set preferences like location, duration, and ad category
  • Go live and start reaching out to real customers!
Ad Specifications
  • Cost: ₹1 per day – that’s it!
  • Visibility: Target specific locations where your potential customers are
  • Ad Validity: Choose how long your ad runs as based on your needs
  • Guided Setup: Simple step-by-step instructions with visuals (attach screenshots or video guide).
Why Choose My Ads?
  • Achieve all your sales in one place
  • Reaches real users in real locations
  • Works with Olopo’s rewards ecosystem
  • Increases visibility without complicated setup
